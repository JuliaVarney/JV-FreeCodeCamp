Carol S. Dweck

Author of Mindset with 2 Million Copies in Print


Carol S. Dweck speaking at TED Talk on the topic of the power in believing you can improve.

Below is a timeline of Carol's life to date:

  • 1946 – Born in New York
  • 1967 – Graduated from Barnard College
  • 1972 – Graduated with her PhD in Psychology from Yale University
  • 1972 – Started her first job at University of Illinois
  • 1981– Became a professor in Harvard’s Laboratory of Human Development
  • 1985 – Returned to University of Illinois as a Professor of Psychology
  • 1989 – Moved to Columbia University as a Professor of Psychology
  • 1998 – Published Motivation and self-regulation across the life span with J. Heckhausen
  • 1999 – Published Self-theories: Their role in motivation, personality and development
  • 2005 – Published Handbook of competence and motivation with A.J. Elliot
  • 2006 – Published Mindset: The new psychology of success
  • 2012 – Published Mindset: How you can fulfill your potential
